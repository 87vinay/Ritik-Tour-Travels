Ritik Tour & Travels for Your Travel Needs in Delhi-NCR

  • 15+ years of experience in the travel industry, serving customers across India, with a strong presence in Delhi NCR.

  • Professional and experienced drivers with extensive knowledge of Indian routes
  • 24/7 customer support

At Ritik Tour & Travels, we pride ourselves on providing exceptional travel experiences across India, with a strong foundation in the Delhi and Ghaziabad region. Since 2010, we've been committed to offering a premium fleet of vehicles and dedicated service to customers throughout the country. From family vacations and corporate travel nationwide to local trips within Delhi and NCR, we've got you covered with our range of comfortable and reliable vehicles. We combine our extensive all-India reach with a deep understanding of the specific travel needs of the Delhi and Ghaziabad community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get Answers to Common Queries

How do I book a vehicle?

You can book a vehicle by contacting us through phone, WhatsApp, or by filling out the enquiry form on our website. Our team will get back to you with availability and pricing details.

What is the payment policy?

We require a 50% advance payment at the time of booking, and the remaining amount can be paid before the journey starts. We accept cash, bank transfers, and digital payment methods.

What is included in the rate?

Our rates include vehicle rental, driver's service, fuel, and vehicle maintenance. Additional costs such as toll taxes, state permits, parking fees, and driver allowance are not included and will be paid by the customer as per our terms.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your booking subject to our cancellation policy. Please inform us at least 48 hours before the scheduled journey for any changes or cancellations.

Are your drivers experienced?

Yes, all our drivers are experienced professionals with valid licenses and extensive knowledge of routes. They are trained to provide excellent customer service and ensure a safe journey.
